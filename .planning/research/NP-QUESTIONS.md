# NP Question Patterns: Nationella Prov Åk 9

**Research type:** Phase Research — NP question patterns for AI tutor system prompts
**Researched:** 2026-04-03
**Subjects:** Biologi (NO), Samhällskunskap (SO), Matematik
**Purpose:** Rewrite ~50-60 AI tutoring system prompts to match real NP experience

---

## Overview: What Makes NP Different from Classroom Tests

Nationella prov for åk 9 differ from regular classroom tests in three key ways:

1. **Standardized quality criteria** — Questions are developed over ~2 years by university research groups (Umeå University for NO, Gothenburg University for SO, PRIM-gruppen/Stockholm University for Matte). They are nationally calibrated against the Lgr11 curriculum's capability requirements (förmågor).

2. **Two-tier question structure** — Every NP combines *faktafrågor* (factual, 1 point each) with *resonerande frågor* (reasoning, 1–3 points each). The reasoning questions are the differentiating factor: E/C/A grades depend on scoring enough reasoning points above the factual minimum.

3. **Authentic contexts** — Questions embed biology, civics, or math in real-world scenarios. Students are not asked to define a term; they are asked to use it to explain something that happens in the real world (övergödning, demokratiska beslut, prisändringar).

**Score structure shared across all three subjects (Lgr11 era):**
- Grade E: ≥ 8 points total (≥ 1–2 from reasoning)
- Grade C: ≥ 12 points total (≥ 3 from reasoning)
- Grade A: ≥ 18 points total (≥ 5 from reasoning)

Total possible: ~39 points (15 factual + 24 reasoning)

## Subject 1: Biologi (NO)

### Exam Structure

| Component | Content | Time |
|-----------|---------|------|
| Delprov A1 | Faktafrågor — short answer/multiple choice on biology concepts | ~90 min total A1+A2 |
| Delprov A2 | Resonerande fråga — one extended question with data/table to interpret and argue from | included above |
| Delprov A3 | Laborationsförberedelse — plan a lab investigation (procedure + materials) | included above |
| Delprov B | Praktisk laboration — conduct and evaluate a lab following instructions | ~90 min |

From 2015 onwards, Delprov B uses the same ready-made lab instructions for all students (no longer linked to A3's individual planning). There is no oral component.

### Question Types

**Faktafrågor (Delprov A1) — 15 questions, 1 point each**

Short-answer or multiple-choice questions testing recall and understanding of biology concepts and processes. Questions are direct but require understanding, not just memorization.

Typical formulations:
- "Vad kallas den process där växter omvandlar solljus till kemisk energi?"
- "Vad menas med biologisk mångfald?"
- "Vilken av följande är en producent i ett ekosystem?"
- "Hur påverkas ekosystemet av överfiske?"
- "Hur många kromosomer har människor normalt?"
- "Vad är skillnaden mellan genotyp och fenotyp?"
- "Vilken roll har fotosyntesen i kretsloppet?"

Topics covered in faktafrågor:
- Ekologi: producenter/konsumenter/nedbrytare, kretslopp, ekosystemtjänster, övergödning, biologisk mångfald
- Kroppen: fotosyntesen, andningssystemet, cirkulationssystemet, nervsystemet, muskler
- Genetik: DNA, gen, allel, dominant/recessiv, fenotyp/genotyp, kromosom, Mendels lagar, Punnettkors
- Evolution: naturligt urval, mutation, adaptation, artbildning
- Cellen: cellens delar och funktioner, skillnad djur-/växtcell

**Resonerande frågor (Delprov A2) — 8 questions, 1–3 points each**

Extended responses where students must apply biological knowledge to analyze, argue, and take a position. A2 often presents a table, graph, or scenario that the student must interpret.

Typical formulations:
- "Diskutera hur en förändring i ett ekosystem kan påverka andra organismer."
- "Hur kan klimatförändringar påverka den biologiska mångfalden?"
- "Resonera kring vikten av att bevara hotade arter."
- "Beskriv hur vi kan minska påverkan av miljögifter på ekosystem och ge exempel på åtgärder."
- "Förklara hur naturligt urval fungerar och ge ett aktuellt exempel."
- "Resonera om hur arv och miljö samverkar för att påverka en individs egenskaper." (enäggstvilling-scenariot är ett klassiskt exempel)
- "Ta ställning: Bör vi vaccinera alla barn? Motivera med biologiska argument."
- "Visa att du kan läsa tabellen och ta ställning till [påstående] med hjälp av biologiska begrepp."

### Difficulty Level

NP Biologi targets the following Lgr11 förmågor:
- Använda biologins begrepp och förklaringsmodeller för att *beskriva och förklara* samband i naturen och kroppen
- Använda biologi för att *granska information, kommunicera och ta ställning* i frågor om miljö och hälsa
- Genomföra *systematiska undersökningar* (Delprov B)

The **reasoning questions** are where most students lose points. An E-level answer names a biological consequence without explanation. A C-level answer explains the mechanism. An A-level answer connects multiple concepts, gives examples, and evaluates implications.

### Tonality

Formal but accessible. Questions use standard biological terminology but embed it in recognizable real-world contexts (Swedish nature, human health, environmental issues). The lab component uses instructional language ("genomför undersökningen", "dokumentera dina observationer").

Swedish phrasing patterns observed in NP Biologi:
- "Resonera kring..." (open reasoning, most common for high-level questions)
- "Beskriv kort..." (factual recall, lower level)
- "Förklara sambandet mellan..." (mechanistic understanding)
- "Ge ett exempel på..." (applied knowledge)
- "Ta ställning till..." (argumentation, A-level)
- "Diskutera..." (analysis from multiple angles)
- "Vad händer med... om...?" (consequence reasoning)
- "Vilken av följande...?" (multiple choice faktafråga)

### Scoring Criteria (Bedömningsanvisningar)

For faktafrågor: one correct answer = 1 point. No partial credit.

For resonerande frågor, points are awarded for:
- **1 point (E-level contribution):** Names relevant biological concept or names a consequence without explanation
- **2 points (C-level contribution):** Explains the mechanism or provides a reasoned argument with biological vocabulary
- **3 points (A-level contribution):** Multi-step reasoning, connects several concepts, evaluates alternatives, uses precise terminology correctly

Critical: The minimum threshold for reasoning points at each grade level means a student who knows all facts but can't reason only reaches E.

### Common Student Mistakes AI Tutoring Should Address

1. **Confusing genotype and phenotype** — Students know the words but swap them under pressure
2. **Missing the mechanism in explanations** — Naming "övergödning orsakar syrebrist" without explaining *why* (algblomning → ökad nedbrytning → syre förbrukas)
3. **Atom/material confusion in kretsloppet** — Students don't understand that atoms cycle but energy flows one way
4. **Evolution misconceptions** — "Arten vill anpassa sig" vs. "individer med fördelaktiga egenskaper överlever och reproducerar sig mer"
5. **Ecosystems treated as isolated** — Not connecting changes between trophic levels
6. **Lab planning (A3)** — Students forget to state what variable they're controlling vs. what they're measuring

## Subject 2: Samhällskunskap (SO)

**Developed by:** Gothenburg University
**Published old exams:** 2016, 2017, 2018 (secrecy expired) — available at gu.se

### Exam Structure

| Component | Content | Time |
|-----------|---------|------|
| Delprov A | Facts + open reasoning questions, with text/table/image stimuli | 120 min |
| Delprov B | Facts + open reasoning questions, different topic focus | 120 min |

Both delprov have the same structure: mix of faktafrågor and resonerande frågor, each with supporting source material (texts, tables, statistics, images). Students use the stimulus material plus their own knowledge.

### Question Types — Documented from Real Published Exams (2013, 2018)

**Type 1: Faktafrågor — Multiple-choice/matching, 1 point each**

Total: ~15 faktafrågor across the exam.

Examples from published exams:
- Vocabulary matching (12 terms paired with definitions):
  - Legal concepts: "juridiskt ansvar", "överklaganderätt", "rättegångsbrott", "vittnesed"
  - Media concepts: "källskydd", "pressubsidier", "presskoden", "offentlighetsprincipen", "propaganda"
  - Political concepts: "demokrati", "ideologi", "monarki", "republik"
- True/false statements (mark whether each påstående stämmer eller inte):
  - "I Sverige är folkomröstningar juridiskt bindande." (Stämmer / Stämmer inte)
  - Series of 11–12 statements about referendums, BNP, HDI, EU membership
- Fill-in-the-blank with word bank: "Fyll i de ord som saknas i texten nedan med hjälp av orden i rutan." (Swedish governance process)
- Multiple-choice selecting 3–4 correct alternatives from 6–9 options
- Identify correct pairings: causes vs. consequences (O/K), direct vs. representative democracy (D/R)

**Type 2: Resonerande frågor — Open response, up to 3 points each**

Total: ~8 resonerande frågor across the exam. These are the grade-determining questions.

Examples from published exams (exact Swedish formulations):

From Delprov A 2018:
- "Välj ut två av dessa och resonera om varför dessa är viktiga för att ett samhälle ska kunna kalla sig demokratiskt." [given a list of democratic principles]
- "Resonera om vilka fördelar... det kan finnas med att det kan ta lång tid att fatta demokratiska beslut."
- "Resonera om fördelar (1-2 st.) och nackdelar (1-2 st.) med att Sveriges befolkning växer."
- "Resonera om orsaker (1-3 st.) till att länder, som Sverige, ger bistånd."
- "Beskriv olika orsaker (1-2 st.) till att personer... får hjälp av en försvarsadvokat."
- "Resonera om vilka fördelar (1-2 st.) och vilka nackdelar (1-2 st.) med att använda sociala medier."

From Delprov B 2013:
- "Ta bort banken ur kretsloppet! Vad händer då med ekonomin i ett land?" [Describe and explain consequences]
- "Vilka andra faktorer kan spela roll i valet av aktivitet?" [given suggested terms: förebild, social bakgrund, kulturell bakgrund, tillgänglighet, ekonomi]
- "Varför är rösträttsåldern 18 år?" [Explain reasoning]
- "Vilka andra villkor bör ett land uppfylla för att det ska vara demokratiskt?" [beyond universal suffrage]
- "Varför tycker FN att utbildning är en viktig metod för att minska fattigdomen?"

From Delprov A 2013:
- "Resonera kring varför det ser ut som tabellen ovan visar." [about income differences by gender and birthplace; suggested terms: kön, etnicitet, yrkesval, fördomar, utbildning]
- "På vilka sätt kan det vara ett samhällsproblem att människor diskrimineras?"
- "På vilka sätt kan konflikter i världen påverka andra länder?" [suggested terms: ekonomi, flyktingar, handel, hot]
- "Vilka anledningar finns det till att vi betalar skatt?"
- "Vilka fördelar och vilka nackdelar kan det finnas med att ha en folkomröstning?"

**Type 3: Source-based questions**

Several questions provide a table, graph, or diagram and ask students to interpret it OR use it as evidence in a reasoning response. The 2013 exam included:
- Income table by gender and birthplace → "Mark two correct statements from six" + "Resonera kring varför det ser ut som tabellen visar"
- Economic circular flow diagram → identify 10 flows (lön, skatt, lån, subvention)
- Population statistics → select 4 correct reasons for urbanization

### Difficulty Level

Faktafrågor test breadth of civic knowledge: Swedish government structure, EU, law concepts, media concepts, economic terms, UN, international relations. Students are expected to know these at a definitional level.

Resonerande frågor test depth: students must construct an argument with 1–3 relevant reasons or examples, connect cause and consequence, see a question from multiple perspectives (ekonomiskt, socialt, politiskt). The key word "resonera" means: name a position + give reasons + use appropriate concepts.

Grade distinctions:
- **E:** Names one relevant reason or consequence without full explanation
- **C:** Explains with a reason + evidence, uses subject-specific language correctly
- **A:** Multiple perspectives, nuanced argument, weighs advantages vs. disadvantages, uses precise societal concepts

### Tonality

Formal but conversational. Questions often embed a real civic situation ("Sveriges befolkning ökar", "Bistånd ges till fattiga länder"). The word "resonera" is the single most important verb. Questions typically specify quantity: "1–2 fördelar", "1–3 orsaker" — this is a scaffolding device to tell students how much to write.

Swedish phrasing patterns in NP Samhällskunskap:
- "Resonera om..." (general reasoning, most common)
- "Resonera om fördelar och nackdelar med..." (balanced argument)
- "Resonera om orsaker till..." (causal reasoning)
- "Beskriv orsaker till att..." (slightly more factual than resonera)
- "Vilka anledningar finns det till att...?" (causal, slightly more open)
- "Vad händer då med... om...?" (consequence reasoning)
- "På vilka sätt kan... påverka...?" (mechanism and connection)
- "Varför...?" (causal explanation)
- "Ta ställning till... Motivera." (argumentation with position)
- "Kryssa för..." (multiple choice)
- "Para ihop..." (matching)
- "Ta bort X... vad händer då?" (thought experiment consequence)
- "Välj ut [N] och resonera om..." (selective argumentation)
- "Resonera kring varför det ser ut som... visar." (data interpretation + causal reasoning)

Topic areas covered across published exams:
- **Demokrati:** principer, folkomröstning, representativ demokrati, partier, riksdag, val
- **Lag och rätt:** juridiska begrepp, rättsprocess, försvarsadvokat, ålder för straffansvar, brottmål/civilmål
- **Ekonomi:** BNP, HDI, utbud/efterfrågan, kretslopp, banker, bistånd, skatter, handel
- **Media och information:** källskydd, presskod, offentlighetsprincipen, sociala medier, propaganda
- **EU och internationella relationer:** EU-regler, FN, konflikter, globalisering, Millennium-mål
- **Samhällsfrågor:** diskriminering, invandring, befolkningstillväxt, urbanisering, rösträttsålder

### Scoring Criteria (Bedömningsanvisningar)

The 2017 bedömningsanvisning from GU confirms:
- Faktafrågor: 1 point each, max 15 points
- Resonerande frågor: up to 3 points each, max 24 points
- Total: 39 points

Grade thresholds:
- E: ≥ 8 points total (varav ≥ 2 from reasoning)
- C: ≥ 12 points (varav ≥ 3 from reasoning)
- A: ≥ 18 points (varav ≥ 5 from reasoning)

For reasoning questions the anvisning provides example answers at each level (E/C/A) — e.g., for a globalization question:
- **E-svar:** Names one advantage (e.g., "bra idéer sprids") without explanation
- **C-svar:** Explains the advantage and notes a tradeoff (e.g., "bra idéer sprids snabbare, vilket kan leda till ekonomisk tillväxt, men risken är att vissa kulturer försvinner")
- **A-svar:** Weighs multiple perspectives with evidence, uses precise economic/social vocabulary, identifies second-order consequences

### Common Student Mistakes AI Tutoring Should Address

1. **Writing too little on resonerande frågor** — Students answer at E-level when C/A is needed; they name a consequence but don't explain it
2. **Not using subject-specific vocabulary** — "Folk är fattiga" instead of "ekonomisk ojämlikhet" or "BNP per capita"
3. **Confusing EU, FN, and Nordic Council** — Which body does what
4. **One-sided answers** — When asked for fördelar och nackdelar, students list only one side
5. **Mixing up juridical concepts** — Brottmål vs. civilmål, åklagare vs. försvarsadvokat
6. **Source analysis weakness** — Students state what the table shows but don't explain why it looks that way
7. **Ignoring the specified quantity** — When asked for "1–2 orsaker", students write either zero explained reasons or a long unfocused essay

## Subject 3: Matematik

**Developed by:** PRIM-gruppen, Stockholm University
**Published old exams:** 2013–2019 (all available, secrecy expired)

### Exam Structure (2025/2026 format — note: oral delprov removed from 2025/26 onwards)

| Delprov | Aid | Time | Content |
|---------|-----|------|---------|
| A (former oral, now removed from 25/26) | — | — | Was oral, now replaced by optional bedömningsstöd |
| B | No calculator, no formula sheet | 40 min | Kortsvars- and flervalsuppgifter |
| C | Calculator + formula sheet | 40 min | Kortsvars- and flervalsuppgifter |
| D | Calculator + formula sheet | 80–100 min | Extended problem-solving, full solutions required |

From 2025/26: the exam is administered over two days (May 11: B+C, May 13: D). The oral component is gone.

### Question Types

**Delprov B — Utan hjälpmedel (kortsvars + flerval)**

Mental math, estimation, number sense, basic algebra without calculator. Tests whether students understand concepts rather than just button-pressing.

Typical problems:
- Calculate with negative numbers, powers, fractions
- Simplify algebraic expressions
- Quick geometry (area, Pythagorean theorem with clean numbers)
- Percentage calculations with round numbers
- Identify which of four statements is correct about a function or equation
- "Vilket påstående stämmer?" multiple choice

**Delprov C — Med hjälpmedel (kortsvars + flerval)**

Same format as B but with calculator. Tests broader mathematical knowledge at recall/application level.

Typical problems:
- Probability: "Hur stor är sannolikheten att...?"
- Statistics: read a diagram, calculate mean/median/mode
- Functions: find the equation of a line from two points
- Geometry: calculate volume of a solid
- Percentage change: a price increases by 15%, what is the new price?

**Delprov D — Med hjälpmedel (fullständiga lösningar)**

The most demanding part. Longer problems requiring multi-step solutions with written reasoning. Students must "visa hur du har kommit fram till dina svar" (show your work). Partial credit is explicitly awarded for progress toward the right answer.

Typical problem types in Delprov D:
- **Real-world word problems:** "En jacka kostar 1 200 kr. Priset sänks med 25%, sedan höjs det sänkta priset med 25%. Beräkna det slutliga priset och förklara varför det skiljer sig från ursprungspriset." [C/A-level: understand that percentages apply to different bases]
- **Functions and samband:** Plot or interpret a linear or quadratic function in a real context, write its equation, explain what the slope means
- **Geometry:** Prove or calculate using Pythagoras, area of composite shapes, volume + surface area
- **Statistics:** Design or analyze a statistical investigation, calculate and interpret spread measures
- **Algebraic reasoning:** Set up and solve equations from a text problem, system of equations
- **Proportionality and ratios:** Mix, scaling, unit conversion

Topic coverage across the exam:
- Taluppfattning (number sense, powers, order of operations)
- Procent (percentage change factors, compound percentage)
- Algebra (equations, expressions, inequalities, factoring)
- Geometri (Pythagorean theorem, area, volume, similarity)
- Funktioner och samband (linear functions, slope, graphs)
- Statistik (diagrams, mean, median, mode, spridning)
- Sannolikhet (simple probability, combined events)

### Difficulty Level

Delprov B: Tests fluency and understanding without computational crutches. A student who only knows "how to press buttons" on a calculator struggles here.

Delprov C: Tests breadth across the curriculum. Most questions have a single correct answer but require multiple steps.

Delprov D: Tests mathematical reasoning and communication. A student who gets the right answer but shows no work gets fewer points than a student who shows a correct method but makes an arithmetic error. Key quote from PRIM's bedömningsprincip: "Utgångspunkten för bedömningen är att eleven ska få poäng för lösningens förtjänster, inte avdrag för fel."

Grade differentiation happens primarily in Delprov D. Students who can set up the correct mathematical model for a word problem and explain their reasoning reach C and A.

### Tonality

Concrete and context-embedded. Real prices, real measurements, real scenarios. The language is plain Swedish with technical math vocabulary. Problems often name a person ("Lena", "Ali", "Maria") doing something concrete.

Swedish phrasing patterns in NP Matematik:
- "Beräkna..." (basic calculation instruction)
- "Visa hur du beräknar..." (show work)
- "Förklara varför..." (conceptual explanation)
- "Rita en graf som visar..." (graphing task)
- "Skriv en ekvation som beskriver sambandet..." (modeling)
- "Vilket av följande påståenden stämmer?" (multiple choice)
- "Hur stor är sannolikheten att...?" (probability)
- "Lös ekvationen..." (algebra)
- "Bestäm arean av..." (geometry)
- "Tabellen visar... Beräkna medelvärdet och medianen." (statistics)
- "En vara kostar X. Priset höjs med Y%. Vad kostar varan nu?" (percentage)
- "Förklara vad lutningen i grafen betyder i det här sammanhanget." (interpretation)
- "Är Jonas' påstående rimligt? Motivera." (reasonableness check)

### Scoring Criteria (Bedömningsanvisningar)

For Delprov B and C kortsvarsuppgifter: only the final answer is graded (no partial credit for method).

For Delprov D: partial credit is explicitly awarded. The bedömningsanvisning specifies:
- Points for setting up the correct equation or model
- Points for correct method even with arithmetic error
- Points for arriving at a reasonable intermediate step
- "Elevsvaret kan avvika något från det korrekta svaret och ändå anses godtagbart"

Grade thresholds for mathematics follow a similar E/C/A structure but are calibrated per exam year. The PRIM-gruppen publishes detailed results reports that show what percentage of students reached each level on each task.

### Common Student Mistakes AI Tutoring Should Address

1. **Percentage confusion** — Applying a 25% decrease and then a 25% increase and expecting to get back to the original price (different base values)
2. **Not showing work in Delprov D** — Getting the right answer but writing nothing, losing potential C/A points
3. **Linear function slope interpretation** — Calculating the slope correctly but not explaining what it means in context ("what does 3 kr/dag mean for this problem?")
4. **Statistical vocabulary** — Confusing medelvärde, median, typvärde under pressure
5. **Pythagoras setup** — Misidentifying the hypotenuse, especially in applied problems
6. **Unit conversion errors** — cm to m, liter to deciliter, especially in volume problems
7. **Word-to-algebra translation** — Students who can solve equations fail to set them up from word problems
8. **Proportionality vs. linearity** — Treating non-proportional relationships as proportional

## Cross-Subject Patterns for AI Tutoring

### The "Resonera" Framework

The word "resonera" signals the key NP skill in all three subjects. A good "resonera"-response always has:
1. A position or claim
2. At least one reason or mechanism
3. A relevant example or evidence
4. (For C/A) A counterpoint, qualification, or connection to another concept

The AI tutor should explicitly ask students to structure their answers this way when practicing reasoning questions.

### Verb Hierarchy by Cognitive Level

| Verb | Level | What it demands |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| "Vad är...?" / "Vilken...?" | E | Recall/recognition |
| "Beskriv..." | E–C | Organized description |
| "Förklara..." | C | Mechanism/causality |
| "Resonera om..." | C–A | Argument with reasons |
| "Analysera..." | C–A | Multi-factor consideration |
| "Diskutera..." | A | Balanced multiple perspectives |
| "Ta ställning... Motivera." | A | Position + defense |
| "Vad händer om...?" | C | Consequence reasoning |

The AI tutor should practice all verb levels but especially push students from "Beskriv" to "Förklara" to "Resonera" — that's the E→C→A progression.

### NP vs Classroom Tests — Key Differences

| Dimension | Classroom test | Nationellt prov |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Scope | One chapter/topic | Entire course (Lgr11 centralt innehåll) |
| Question style | Teacher-specific, varies widely | Nationally standardized, consistent format |
| Context embedding | Often abstract | Always in real/authentic context |
| Two-tier structure | Often only recall | Always fact + reasoning layers |
| Partial credit | Teacher discretion | Explicit bedömningsanvisning |
| Stakes | Internal grade input | Official grade-setting input |
| Vocabulary requirement | Varies | Ämnesspecifikt språkbruk explicitly required for C/A |

- Ekologi: ekosystem, kretslopp, övergödning, biologisk mångfald, ekosystemtjänster, miljögifter, hållbarhet
- Kroppen: andning, cirkulation, nervsystemet, hormoner, immunsystemet, reproduktion
- Genetik: DNA, gen, allel, fenotyp/genotyp, Mendel, dominant/recessiv, mutation, ärftliga sjukdomar

## Implications for AI Tutor System Prompts

### Per Subject

**Biologi system prompts should:**
- Use the two-tier format: start with faktafråga-style questions (recall/recognition), then escalate to resonerande fråga-style (apply/analyze)
- Use real Swedish ecological and biological contexts: övergödning i svenska sjöar, havsörn, enäggstvillingar, vaccination
- Push students to explain mechanisms, not just name them: "Du sa att övergödning är dåligt — förklara vad som faktiskt händer steg för steg"
- Address the common evolution misconception proactively: organisms don't "want" to adapt

**Samhällskunskap system prompts should:**
- Build the "resonera"-skill explicitly: guide students through position → reason → example → counterpoint
- Teach vocabulary in context, not as a list: embed "BNP", "offentlighetsprincipen", "rättsstat" in questions
- Require students to give the specific number of reasons the question asks for (1–2, 1–3)
- Use Swedish civic contexts: folkomröstningen om euron, riksdag, kommunal vs. statlig verksamhet

**Matematik system prompts should:**
- Mirror Delprov D style for reasoning exercises: word-problem format, require showing work
- Build percentage understanding using the "different base value" concept explicitly
- Ask for interpretations, not just calculations: "Vad betyder lutningen i det här sammanhanget?"
- Address unit conversions and proportionality vs. linearity confusions directly

### Universal Principles from NP Pattern Research

1. **Authentic contexts always** — No abstract questions ("Define photosynthesis"). Instead: "Varför är fotosyntesen viktig för syrehalten i sjön?"
2. **Scaffold with quantity** — "Ge 1–2 exempel" signals expected depth without being prescriptive
3. **Subject-specific vocabulary required for C/A** — The tutor should explicitly ask students to use rätt begrepp and correct them when they avoid it
4. **Reasoning over recall** — The exam gives most points for reasoning; the tutor should mirror this by spending most dialogue on reasoning questions
5. **Show-your-work culture** — In math especially, the tutor should always ask "Hur tänkte du?" even when the answer is correct
