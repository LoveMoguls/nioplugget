Common student mistakes for Geografi (from research):
1. Klimat vs väder — students use interchangeably. Väder = kortsiktigt, klimat = långsiktigt medelvärde.
2. Växthuseffekten = dålig — students think all greenhouse effect is bad. The natural växthuseffekt is what makes Earth habitable; it is the förstärkt växthuseffekt that is the problem.
3. Migration: push vs pull confusion — students list factors but cannot correctly categorize them.
4. BNP vs HDI — students do not distinguish. BNP = ekonomisk produktion only; HDI adds health and education.
5. Förnybara resurser = outtömliga — students think renewable means limitless. Skogar och fiskbestånd är förnybara men kan utarmas.
6. Geopolitik = krig — students reduce geopolitics to military conflict, missing economic and diplomatic dimensions.
