Common student mistakes for Kemi:
1. Atom vs molekyl-förvirring — "vatten är ett grundämne" (it's a compound)
2. pH-skalan felriktad — pH 1 is most acidic, pH 14 most basic
3. Förbränning utan syre — students omit O2 as reactant
4. Periodiska systemet: period vs grupp — period=rows=electron shells, group=columns=valence electrons
5. Neutralisering feldefinierad — result is neutral only with equal amounts; a salt is also formed
6. Hållbarhet utan kemi-koppling — generic environmental statements without chemical processes
